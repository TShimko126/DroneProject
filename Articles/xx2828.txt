Monday's developments

l Egypt proposed a temporary cease-fire in the most serious attempt yet by
international mediators to intervene in the week-old conflict.

l Israel shot down a drone launched by Hamas. It was the first deployment of
such a craft into Israeli airspace.

l The death toll from seven days of Israeli airstrikes in Gaza rose to more than
185.

l Secretary of State John F. Kerry is expected to shuttle among Mideast capitals
this week in a bid to end fighting, news reports said.
